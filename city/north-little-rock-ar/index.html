Is North Little Rock, AR Safe?

North Little Rock, AR has below-average safety with a score of 52/100, placing it more dangerous than 98% of US cities. The city ranks #8,725 out of 8,874 US cities in our safety database.

The violent crime rate of 1180.1 per 100k is 211% above the national average (380 per 100k). Violent crime accounts for 50% of all reported incidents.
