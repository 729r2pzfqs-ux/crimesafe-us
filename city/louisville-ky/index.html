Is Louisville, KY Safe?

Louisville, KY has below-average safety with a score of 58/100, placing it more dangerous than 50% of US cities. The city ranks #0 out of 8,874 US cities in our safety database.

The violent crime rate of 707.4 per 100k is 86% above the national average (380 per 100k). Violent crime accounts for approximately 18% of all reported incidents.

Exercise additional caution, especially at night. Be aware of your surroundings and secure valuables.
