Is Boston, MA Safe?

Boston, MA has average safety with a score of 62/100, placing it around the middle of US cities. The city ranks among 8,874 US cities.

The violent crime rate of 627.9 per 100k is 65% above the national average (380 per 100k). Violent crime accounts for 24% of all incidents.

Most residents feel safe day-to-day. Standard urban precautions are recommended.
