Is Centerville, IA Safe?

Centerville, IA has average safety with a score of 73/100, placing it around the middle of US cities. The city ranks #6,056 out of 8,874 US cities in our safety database.

The violent crime rate of 263.0 per 100k is 31% below the national average (380 per 100k). Violent crime accounts for 50% of all reported incidents.

Most residents feel safe day-to-day. Standard urban precautions are recommended.
